Introduction

The aim of this programming assignment is to write a Haskell program that translates a predicate logic formula into its prenex conjunctive normal form.

A formula is in prenex conjunctive normal form (PCNF) if it is of the form 

       Q1x1 ... Qnxn M

where the Qi are quantifiers and M is a quantifier-free formula in CNF. The (possibly empty) sequence Q1x1 ... Qnxn is the prefix and M is the matrix [Ben-Ari (2012), Definition 9.9 - van Dalen (2013), Definition 3.5.10]. Every formula in predicate logic can be transformed into an equivalent formula in PCNF [van Dalen (2013), Theorem 3.5.11].
